    🧠 My Design Process

    01

    Theme & Emotion

    Define the emotional experience the player should feel.

    02

    Core Mechanic

    Create primary gameplay interactions that support the theme.

    03

    Player Experience

    Plan how the player discovers, learns, and master mechanics.

    Build Systems

    Expand the mechanic into challenges and progression.

    05

    Iterate

    Improve the design through testing and feedback loops.
